Bachelor of Business Management Hons. (BBM)

The Bachelor of Business Management programme aims at developing the business knowledge and related skills in undergraduates, essential for the modern day corporate world. It has been designed to provide its students with the necessary theoretical foundation to business management and value additions such as global training opportunities, industry interaction, research exposure and personality development required of future business leaders.

The programme is best suited for those who expect to be employed in a managerial role in a business environment . This will also provide a strong academic foundation for postgraduate studies as the university provides a highly conducive environment for research engagements . Individuals with interest in entrepreneurial enterprises and community movements are also encouraged to apply.



Programme Structure

 Accumulation of 90 credits (Year 3): BBM (General Degree)

 Accumulation of 60 credits (Year2): HND in Business Management

 Accumulation of 30 credits (Year 1): Diploma in Business Management








Bachelor of Science in Information Technology Hons. (BSc IT)

BSc in Information Technology provides its students with a thorough understanding of the technology and the operational workings of IT on which the current world and its industries and enterprises depend on. Today IT has become an integral part of our daily lives, our health , finance and educational systems ,and evolutionary breakthroughs that define the future of mankind.

This programme prepares its students to be engaged as IT professionals, entrepreneurs and inventors contributing to the technological advancements of the times. It consists of essential theoretical practices coupled up with highly effective practical exercises throughout the period of study. All students go through a mandatory internship of six months during the third year.



Programme Structure

 Accumulation of 120 credits (Year 4): BSc IT (Hons)

 Accumulation of 90 credits (Year 3): BSc IT (General Degree)

 Accumulation of 60 credits (Year 2): HND in Information Technology

 Accumulation of 30 credits (Year 1): Diploma in Information Technology
